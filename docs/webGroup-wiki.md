## Backlink Campaign Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Confirmed online | The campaign record marks the placement as live or visible after posting. |
| Needs recheck | The submission produced a comment URL, moderation state, or partial evidence, but public visibility was not fully confirmed in the export. |
| Failed | The target page did not produce a usable placement, usually because no open form was found or the submission did not persist. |
| Skipped | The page was intentionally skipped, commonly because of login walls, captcha, moderation risk, or poor fit. |
